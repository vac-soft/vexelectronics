Shipping Policy

V Ex Electronics Systems Pvt Ltd

At V Ex Electronics Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to providing fast and reliable shipping to our customers. Please review the following shipping policy to understand our delivery process.

Shipping Rates

Our shipping rates are calculated based on the weight and destination of the package. We offer flat rates for standard shipping, and expedited shipping is available at an additional cost.

Delivery Time

We make every effort to process and ship your order within 24-48 hours. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. Delivery times will vary depending on the shipping method selected and the destination of the package.

Delivery Options

We offer multiple delivery options, including standard and expedited shipping. Standard shipping usually takes between 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping typically takes 2-3 business days. Customers can choose their preferred shipping method at checkout.

Tracking Information

We provide tracking information for all orders so you can monitor the progress of your shipment. Please note that it may take up to 24 hours for the tracking information to update.

Returns and Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free returns and refunds policy. Please refer to our returns policy for more information.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently only offer shipping within India. Additionally, we cannot ship to PO boxes or APO/FPO addresses. If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping policy, please contact us at admin@vexeelctronics.in or +91 44 43512000.
